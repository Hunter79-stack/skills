# Launch: Ship and Get Paid

## The Reality

Launch is not a moment. It's a habit.

One launch reaches ~10% of potential audience. Ten launches reach 65%. Launch the same product multiple times per year.

## Minimum Viable Launch

1. Post on Twitter/X: "I made X. Here's the link."
2. Post on relevant community (Indie Hackers, subreddit, HN)
3. Email your list (even if it's 50 people)

That's it. Stop overcomplicating.

The only real validation is money.

## Founding Member Offer

What works:
- **40-50% off** (not free forever â€” that's a trap)
- **Real limit**: "First 100" that's actually 100
- **Extra value**: Direct access, input on roadmap, badge
- **Lock-in**: "Keep this price while subscribed"

What to avoid:
- Unlimited lifetime deals (destroys future pricing)
- Fake scarcity (people can tell)
- Countdown timers that reset
