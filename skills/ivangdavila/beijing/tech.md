## üí° Career Advice

### For New Arrivals

1. **Learn survival Chinese** ‚Äî Even basics help
2. **Build WeChat network** ‚Äî Professional networking happens here
3. **Start with MNC or big tech** ‚Äî Better visa support, easier adjustment
4. **Join communities** ‚Äî Meetups, online groups
5. **Be patient** ‚Äî Takes 6-12 months to understand market

### For Career Growth

1. **Learn professional Chinese** ‚Äî Opens more doors
2. **Understand local business** ‚Äî Not just tech
3. **Build mixed network** ‚Äî Chinese and international
4. **Consider startup later** ‚Äî After understanding ecosystem
5. **Stay current** ‚Äî China tech moves fast

### Red Flags

- Company can't explain visa sponsorship
- Salary paid in cash or offshore
- "We'll fix the visa later"
- No HR department
- Unlicensed "consulting" arrangement
