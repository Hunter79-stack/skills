## Food Crawl Routes

### South Mumbai Heritage (3 hours)
1. Britannia (Berry Pulao lunch) → 
2. Walk through Fort → 
3. Kyani (chai + khari) →
4. Walk to Chowpatty →
5. Sardar (Pav bhaji dinner)

### Bandra Evening (3 hours)
1. Elco (pani puri) →
2. Walk Linking Road →
3. Pali Village Cafe (drinks) →
4. Carter Road sunset →
5. Candies (dinner)

### Street Food Crawl (2-3 hours)
1. Juhu Beach (bhel) →
2. Anand Vada Pav (Vile Parle) →
3. Amar Juice Centre (pav bhaji)

### Non-Veg Night (2-3 hours)
Mohammed Ali Road: Start at 7pm, hit multiple stalls
1. Seekh kebabs (any stall)
2. Nalli nihari (Sarvi)
3. Chicken (Noor Mohammadi)
4. Malpua (Suleman Usman)
