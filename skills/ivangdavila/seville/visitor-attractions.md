# Attractions — Sevilla

## Must-See (Essential)

### 1. Real Alcazar
Moorish palace complex. Stunning gardens and architecture.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Why** | Arguably Spain's most beautiful palace |
| **Time** | 2-3 hours |
| **Book** | REQUIRED — book 2+ weeks ahead |
| **Cost** | EUR 14.50 (general), EUR 6 (students) |
| **When** | Morning to avoid heat and crowds |
| **Tip** | Skip the Royal Apartments add-on |

**Warning:** Without advance booking, expect 2-3 hour queue. Book at alcazarsevilla.org.

---

### 2. Catedral and Giralda
World's largest Gothic cathedral. Columbus's tomb. Iconic bell tower.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Why** | UNESCO site, Giralda views |
| **Time** | 1.5-2 hours |
| **Book** | Recommended (skip line) |
| **Cost** | EUR 12 |
| **When** | Early morning or late afternoon |
| **Tip** | Free Monday 16:30-18:00 (queue early) |

**The Giralda:** 35 ramps (not stairs) to the top. Easy climb, best city views.

---

### 3. Plaza de Espana
1929 Expo pavilion. Stunning semi-circular plaza with tiled alcoves.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Why** | Iconic, photogenic, free |
| **Time** | 30-60 minutes |
| **Book** | Not needed |
| **Cost** | FREE |
| **When** | Sunset or morning |
| **Tip** | Row boats on the canal (EUR 6/35 min) |

**Hidden feature:** Each alcove represents a Spanish province with historical tiles.

---

### 4. Barrio Santa Cruz
Medieval Jewish quarter. White walls, narrow streets, hidden plazas.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Why** | Atmosphere, getting lost |
| **Time** | 1-2 hours wandering |
| **Book** | N/A |
| **Cost** | FREE |
| **When** | Morning or evening |
| **Tip** | Skip the tourist restaurants |

**Best spots:** Plaza de los Venerables, Callejon del Agua, Plaza de Dona Elvira.

---

### 5. Metropol Parasol (Setas)
Modern wooden structure. Rooftop walkway with city views.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Why** | Modern architecture, views |
| **Time** | 30-45 minutes |
| **Book** | Optional (skip line) |
| **Cost** | EUR 5 (includes drink) |
| **When** | Sunset |
| **Tip** | Free from below, only pay for rooftop |

**Underground:** Roman ruins in basement (Antiquarium, EUR 4).

---

## Highly Recommended

### Triana Bridge and Neighborhood
Cross the river, explore authentic Sevilla.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Why** | Real neighborhood, ceramics, flamenco roots |
| **Time** | 2-3 hours |
| **Cost** | FREE |
| **Tip** | Visit the ceramic shops on Calle Alfareria |

---

### Centro de Flamenco (Museo del Baile Flamenco)
Small museum about flamenco history.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Cost** | EUR 10 |
| **Why** | Context before seeing live flamenco |
| **Skip if** | Not interested in flamenco history |

---

### Archivo de Indias
Colonial-era documents archive. UNESCO site.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Cost** | FREE |
| **Why** | Original documents from conquest era |
| **Time** | 30-45 minutes |

---

### Museo de Bellas Artes
Spain's second-best art museum after Prado.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Cost** | EUR 1.50 (free for EU citizens) |
| **Why** | Murillo, Zurbaran, Spanish masters |
| **Time** | 1.5-2 hours |

---

### Casa de Pilatos
Lesser-known palace with stunning tiles and patios.

| Info | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Cost** | EUR 10 (ground floor) / EUR 12 (full) |
| **Why** | Beautiful, less crowded than Alcazar |
| **Time** | 1 hour |
| **Tip** | Great alternative if Alcazar sold out |

## Timing Tips

### Best Order (1 Day)
1. **08:00** — Alcazar (first slot, least crowded)
2. **11:00** — Cathedral and Giralda
3. **13:00** — Lunch tapas in Santa Cruz or Centro
4. **15:00** — Rest (siesta time, things close)
5. **18:00** — Plaza de Espana
6. **20:00** — Sunset at Setas or Triana bridge
7. **21:00** — Tapas dinner in Triana

### Booking Priority
1. **Alcazar** — Book 2+ weeks ahead (sells out)
2. **Cathedral** — Book 2-3 days ahead (skip line)
3. **Setas** — Same day usually fine

### Free Days
- **Cathedral**: Monday 16:30-18:00
- **Alcazar**: Monday 18:00-19:00 (Apr-Sep), 16:00-17:00 (Oct-Mar)
- **Museo de Bellas Artes**: Free for EU citizens always
