## Seasonal Adjustments

### Summer (Jul-Aug)
- Start at 08:00 (museums open)
- Indoor activities 12:00-18:00
- Evening focus 20:00+
- Pool time mid-afternoon
- Expect some closures

### Winter (Dec-Feb)
- Start later (09:30)
- More indoor time
- Earlier dinners possible
- Christmas markets (Dec)

### Semana Santa (Easter)
- Book EVERYTHING 6+ months ahead
- Follow procession schedules
- Emotional, intense atmosphere
- 3-4x accommodation prices
- Worth it if planned

### Feria de Abril (2 weeks after Easter)
- Feria grounds essential
- Day activities still possible
- Evening = Feria
- Book months ahead
- Flamenco dress encouraged
