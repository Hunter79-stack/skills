# Activities — Couple

## Date Ideas by Budget

### Free / Under €10
- Picnic with homemade food
- Sunset/sunrise watching
- Cook challenge (same ingredients, different dishes)
- Photo walk in new neighborhood
- Home spa night
- Stargazing
- Board game tournament

### €10-50
- Museum + coffee
- Farmers market + cook together
- Movie marathon with themed snacks
- Mini golf or arcade
- Hiking + packed lunch
- Art class (pottery, painting)

### €50-150
- Nice dinner out
- Concert or live show
- Day trip to nearby town
- Couples massage
- Cooking class
- Escape room

### €150+
- Weekend getaway
- Hot air balloon
- Fine dining experience
- Spa day together
- Adventure activity (skydiving, diving)

## Gift Ideas by Style

### Experience-Lover
- Concert/show tickets
- Class together (cooking, dance, art)
- Trip or weekend away
- Adventure booking

### Physical-Gift Person
- Something they mentioned wanting (track in ideas.md)
- Upgrade of something they use daily
- Handmade/personalized item
- Quality version of something they buy cheap

### Words-Person
- Handwritten letter
- Photo book with captions
- Video compilation with messages
- Custom song or poem

## Long-Distance Specific

### Virtual Dates
- Watch party (same movie synced)
- Cook same recipe together on video
- Online game night
- Virtual museum tour
- Read same book, discuss

### Surprise Ideas
- Delivery to their door (food, flowers)
- Letters arriving on special days
- Care package with "open when" notes
- Playlist with voice notes between songs

### Countdown Activities
- Daily voice note until reunion
- Shared journal entries
- Photo-a-day challenge
- Planning next visit together

## New Relationship Phase

### Getting to Know Each Other
- Questions games (not generic ones)
- Share playlists, swap favorite albums
- Cook family recipes for each other
- Visit each other's favorite spots
- Meet each other's friends naturally

### Creating Traditions
- Weekly date night (specific day)
- Monthly new-restaurant exploration
- Seasonal activities (summer picnics, winter markets)
- Morning or evening ritual

## Rekindling Ideas (Long-Term)

### Break the Routine
- Surprise date (plan without telling)
- Role-play "first date" — pretend meeting again
- Try activity from early relationship
- New shared hobby

### Intentional Connection
- Weekly check-in conversation
- Gratitude practice (daily appreciation)
- Dream/bucket list session
- Phone-free evenings
