# Milestones — Couple

## Milestone Types

### Standard Milestones
Track these in `~/couple/timeline.md`:
- Anniversary (main)
- Partner's birthday
- First date anniversary
- Move-in anniversary
- Engagement/wedding dates

### Micro-Milestones
Often forgotten but meaningful:
- First "I love you"
- First trip together
- First concert/event
- Meeting families
- First home purchase
- Adopting a pet together

## Special Milestone Planning

### Decade Anniversaries (10, 20, 25+)
- Start planning 3+ months ahead
- Consider: trip, party, renewal ceremony
- Create memory compilation (photos, letters, timeline)

### After Major Life Events
When resuming after kids/career changes:
- "Restart" date — recreate early relationship energy
- New traditions that fit current life
- Acknowledgment of growth together
